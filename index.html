Student projects

In the Algorithms and Complexity group at IT University of Copenhagen, we offer students a variety of different topics for their research projects as well as for their Bachelor's or Master's thesis. These range from algorithm engineering (implementing and testing algorithms), over theory (coming up with new results in algorithms and complexity), to visualization (visualizing existing algorithms, mostly for pedagogical purposes), and others!
